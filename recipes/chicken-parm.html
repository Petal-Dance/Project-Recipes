Homemade Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is a popular dish in many Italian restaurants. On top of being a delicious dish, it's also easy to make at home. Depending on what restaurant you go to, the recipe is going to vary. This recipe in particular is crafted in such a way that the sauce doesn't overwhelm the chicken. With using breading, cheese, and a little bit of sauce on the top, this chicken parmesan will be a delicious meal. Don't worry if you want more sauce, extra sauce can be served on the side.
